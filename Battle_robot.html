Battle Robot (Final Project for ME340)


Summary

This project was for ME 340,Mechnical Engineering Design Methodology. We were put into teams of 4 and were tasked with designing and making a sumo battle robot. We were given a base set of parts, 20 USD to spend on the robot, and allowed to use any prexisting parts we had to build our robot. Our team was one of two robots that had some type of spinning weponary to use offensively. We ultimately didn't win the competition but, we learn a lot about project management, design or a robot, and control algrothms. This project also cemented my intrest in mechatronic systems.
